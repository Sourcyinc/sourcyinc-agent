## Pricing – Compare Features

The following comparison applies exclusively to the **Pricing** section and is intended to help you understand which type of agent best fits your business needs.

---

### Voice Capabilities
- **Text Agent:** Not designed for voice conversations.
- **Voice Agent:** Handles real phone conversations with natural tone and flow.
- **Bundle:** Uses voice only when conversation truly matters.

---

### Conversational Context (Session-Based)
- **Text Agent:** Maintains context only within the active chat session.
- **Voice Agent:** Maintains context only within the active call.
- **Bundle:** Context is preserved per interaction, based on the channel being used.

> Context is not stored or reused across different conversations.

---

### SMS & Email Automation
- **Text Agent:** Ideal for notifications, follow-ups, and quick responses.
- **Voice Agent:** Limited — voice is not ideal for written communication.
- **Bundle:** Uses text for consistency and voice for conversation.

---

### CRM Two-Way Sync
- **Text Agent:** Reads and updates CRM data during text interactions.
- **Voice Agent:** Accesses CRM data in real time while speaking with callers.
- **Bundle:** Works across both channels depending on how the client engages.

---

### Client Data Usage
- **Text Agent:** Uses CRM and data sources to answer written questions.
- **Voice Agent:** Uses the same data to guide live conversations.
- **Bundle:** Data is available regardless of the interaction type.

---

### Inbound Handling
- **Text Agent:** Handles incoming messages and written requests.
- **Voice Agent:** Answers inbound phone calls automatically.
- **Bundle:** Covers inbound communication across voice and text.

---

### Outbound Engagement
- **Text Agent:** Effective for reminders and written follow-ups.
- **Voice Agent:** Designed for outbound qualification and conversations.
- **Bundle:** Combines outreach with real-time conversation.

---

### Outbound Dialing
- **Text Agent:** Not supported.
- **Voice Agent:** Places outbound calls and handles live interactions.
- **Bundle:** Uses outbound calls only when a conversation is required.

---

### Lead Qualification
- **Text Agent:** Quick, structured qualification through messaging.
- **Voice Agent:** Deeper qualification through live conversation.
- **Bundle:** Matches qualification depth to the channel.

---

### Decision Handling
- **Text Agent:** Executes decisions based on rules and inputs.
- **Voice Agent:** Adjusts responses dynamically during calls.
- **Bundle:** Decision flow adapts to interaction type.

---

### Human Interaction Style
- **Text Agent:** Clear, concise, and professional.
- **Voice Agent:** Natural, conversational, and human-like.
- **Bundle:** Lets users interact the way they prefer.

---

### Use-Case Alignment
- **Text Agent:** Best for quick answers and status checks.
- **Voice Agent:** Best for discussions and clarifications.
- **Bundle:** Covers both without forcing one channel.

---

### Availability
- **Text Agent:** Available 24/7.
- **Voice Agent:** Available 24/7.
- **Bundle:** Always available without staffing limitations.

---

### Call Recording & Transcripts
- **Text Agent:** Not applicable.
- **Voice Agent:** Calls are recorded and transcribed.
- **Bundle:** Full visibility into voice interactions.

---

### Knowledge Base (RAG)
- **Text Agent:** Answers using your connected documents and data.
- **Voice Agent:** Explains the same data during live calls.
- **Bundle:** One data source, multiple interaction formats.

---

### Workflow Automation
- **Text Agent:** Executes predefined workflows via text.
- **Voice Agent:** Triggers workflows during calls.
- **Bundle:** Automation adapts to the interaction.

---

### Experience Control
- **Text Agent:** Predictable and consistent.
- **Voice Agent:** Flexible and adaptive.
- **Bundle:** Balanced control across channels.

---

### Scalability
- **Text Agent:** Scales instantly with demand.
- **Voice Agent:** Scales conversations without hiring.
- **Bundle:** Scales operations, not headcount.

---

### Dedicated Development
- **Text Agent:** Not included.
- **Voice Agent:** Not included.
- **Bundle:** Priority customization and development support.

---

### Best Fit
- **Text Agent:** Businesses focused on speed and efficiency.
- **Voice Agent:** Businesses that rely on conversation and trust.
- **Bundle:** Businesses that need both.

---

### Setup Time ⓘ
**All plans:** **5–10 Business Days**
- **Bundle:** Varies

> *Time from contract to live deployment.  
> Includes agent configuration, knowledge base setup, CRM connections, and testing to ensure reliable performance.*
